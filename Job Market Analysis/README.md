# 📊 Job Market Analysis Dashboard

## 📌 Overview
This project presents a Job Market Analysis Dashboard built using Power BI.  
It provides insights into job postings, applicant trends, experience requirements, and industry distribution.

---

## 📂 Dataset Features

The dataset includes the following columns:

- Job Posting ID  
- Job Posting Date  
- Job Title  
- Job Title Full  
- Job Position Type  
- Job Position Level  
- Years of Experience  
- Job Skills  
- Job Location  
- Minimum Pay  
- Maximum Pay  
- Pay Rate  
- Number of Applicants  
- Company Name  
- Company Industry  
- Company Size  

---

## 📊 Key Insights

### 📈 Job Trends
- Job postings increased from 2018 to 2021
- 2021 shows the highest hiring activity

### 👩‍💻 Applicant Analysis
- Total applicants: 209
- Increasing competition in recent years

### 🎯 Experience Level
- Average experience required: 2.67 years
- Most roles are Junior and Mid-level

### 💼 Job Roles
- High demand for:
  - Software Engineer
  - Data Analyst
  - Data Engineer

### 🏢 Company Size Impact
- Larger companies receive more applicants
- Smaller companies have lower competition

### 🌍 Location Insights
- Majority of jobs are located in the United States

### 🏭 Industry Analysis
- Education Management shows notable hiring trends

### ⏳ Job Type
- Most job postings are Full-time
