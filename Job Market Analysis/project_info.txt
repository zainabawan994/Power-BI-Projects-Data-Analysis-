Project: Job Market Analysis Dashboard

This project analyzes job market trends using a dataset of job postings. 
The dashboard provides insights into job demand, applicant trends, experience requirements, and industry distribution.

Key Highlights:

- Job postings increased significantly from 2018 to 2021, showing growth in the job market.
- Total applicants recorded: 209.
- Average required experience is 2.67 years, indicating demand for junior and mid-level roles.
- Most common job roles include Software Engineer, Data Analyst, and Data Engineer.
- Larger companies attract more applicants compared to smaller companies.
- Majority of job postings are full-time positions.
- Jobs are mostly located in the United States.
- Education Management industry shows notable hiring activity.

Issues:

- Salary-related fields (Minimum and Maximum Pay) contain missing or blank values.
- Some visualizations may require improvement for better clarity.

Tools Used:

- Power BI (Dashboard creation)
- Dataset with job postings information

Purpose:

To understand hiring trends, job demand, and skill requirements in the modern job market.
